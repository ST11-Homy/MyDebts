Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2025

My Debts is an offline mobile application designed to help users manage personal or business-related debt records. We value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

My Debts does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally. All the information you enter in the app is saved locally on your device. The types of data you may choose to enter include:

  • Customer or client’s first and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Business name (optional)
  • Address (optional)
  • Debt records such as amount, status, and date

2. Offline Operation

This app operates fully offline. It does not require an internet connection to function and does not send any user data to our servers or any third-party services.

3. Data Storage and Security

All information is stored securely on your local device storage. We do not have access to this information. You are solely responsible for backing up or deleting your data as needed.

Please note that if your device is lost or data is deleted, we cannot recover your information.

4. No Third-Party Services

My Debts does not use or integrate any third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Advertising platforms (e.g., AdMob)
  • Cloud storage or syncing services

Your data never leaves your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has used this app and entered personal data, please contact us and we will assist accordingly.

6. User Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You may view, edit, or delete your entries within the app at any time.
  • We do not track usage, access your entries, or request permissions unrelated to the app’s offline features.

7. Intellectual Property

All icons, illustrations, animations, and other media used in My Debts are sourced from publicly available open-source or free-to-use repositories such as Flaticon, Freepik, Font Awesome, and LottieFiles. Wherever attribution is required, it has been properly included in the app’s “Credit & Acknowledgement” screen as specified by the original license agreements.

The app does not claim ownership over these assets and respects all licensing terms set by their respective authors and platforms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal changes. Any updates will be available within the app or on the app’s listing page, along with the new effective date.
